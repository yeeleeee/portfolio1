Hou-yi and Ten Suns
This project is an interactive game featuring an ancient Chinese character Ho-Yi. The game is created in Unity 3D, and exported as WebGL, HTML, CSS and JavaScript, which is embedded into our website.
About the project
Duration: 6 weeks
Team size: 6
Reaches: 50

We aimed to have a storytelling project joined with a VR game to make it more engaging. I contributed as a UI and sound designer, ensuring the consistency of experience across the platforms - web and VR.

The game is based on a Chinese myth, in which a hero named Houyi shoots down 9 of the 10 suns to save his people from suffering from the heat. The story has three different endings, and users can experience those different narratives based on their performance in the game.
Starting from a storyboard, we made a physical environment to make three dimensional one.
